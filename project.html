Frequently Asked Questions

The mission of Aroc is to foster creativity, ignite inspiration and build communities through the transformative power of the art.

There are several ways to get involved with our organization, You can become a member, Volunteer your time and skills or even donate to support our initiatives.

Yes, becomingg a member comes with several benefits. Members receive exclusive inivitations to exhibitions and events, discounts on workshops, talks, mentorship programs and access to member-only resources.

Aroc provides resources such as studio spaces, access to speciallised equipment, grant opportunities and professional development resources to support the artistic practice and career growth.

The prices listed for our artworks are set by the artists and are generally not negotiable. They reflect the value of the artwork and the artist's expertise.

We actively collaborate with galleries, theaters and cultural institutions to create interdiscplinary programs, joint exhibitions and exchanges, these collaborations enrich the cultural landscape creating meanful experiences for the audience.

We engage with art collectors and patrons through exclusive events, private viewings and personalized experiences. we also offer opportunities for collectors to connect with artists and acquire unique pieces for their collection.

We welcome talented and emerging artists to join our platform. To get started, sennd an Email or visit our "Contact" page and submit your portfolio. Our team will review your work, and if it aligns with our platform, we'll get in touch with you ASAP.
